# Branding

The Phantom Mirror project identity remains reserved to the project maintainer.

This includes, without limitation:

- the name `Phantom Mirror`
- the app name shown in the UI
- the logo
- the app icon
- UI branding
- graphics and visual identity
- other assets that identify an official Phantom Mirror build or release

These identity elements are not licensed for reuse under `LICENSE.md`.

## What this means

- Forks, private builds, and derivative projects may exist for local review and
  development.
- Public redistribution of the source code or build artifacts is not permitted
  under `LICENSE.md` without prior written permission from the maintainer.
- They may not reuse the Phantom Mirror identity in a way that suggests they
  are official Phantom Mirror releases.
- Anyone publishing their own version must use a different name, different
  icons, and different branding, and must clearly state that it is not an
  official Phantom Mirror release.

## Allowed references

References to Phantom Mirror for purposes of attribution, compatibility,
history, or origin are allowed as long as they do not create confusion about
source, sponsorship, or official status.

Examples of acceptable references include:

- `Based on Phantom Mirror`
- `Compatible with Phantom Mirror settings`
- `Forked from Phantom Mirror`

## Official downloads

Official Phantom Mirror downloads are published only through the official
sources named in `README.md` and `RELEASES.md`.

This file does not claim that Phantom Mirror is a registered trademark, and no
such claim should be made in project materials unless that legal status changes
in the future.
